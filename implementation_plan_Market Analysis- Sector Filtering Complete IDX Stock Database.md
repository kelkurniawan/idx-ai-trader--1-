# Market Analysis: Sector Filtering + Complete IDX Stock Database

Add business sector filtering to the Market Analysis tab and expand the stock database to include ~100 registered IDX stocks across all IDX-IC sectors, sourced from official stock indices.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Data Source**: Stock list is compiled from official IDX indices — **LQ45**, **IDX30**, **IDX80**, and **KOMPAS100**. These represent the most liquid, actively traded stocks on the Indonesian Stock Exchange.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Sector Classification**: Using **IDX-IC** (IDX Industrial Classification, replaced JASICA in 2021) with **11 sectors** (excluding "Listed Investment Products" which is ETFs/funds, not tradeable stocks for analysis).
